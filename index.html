Problem Statement
Merchants pay flat rates with no loyalty incentive.
Razorpay bleeds its highest-margin revenue.
Merchant Pain Points
💰

High transaction costs

2% standard, 2.2% no-code, 3% international — no volume discounts exist

⚖️

No volume-based incentives

Flat-rate model doesn't reward merchants who process higher volumes

🔄

Easy to compare & switch

Per-txn pricing makes cost comparison trivial. Multi-PG is the default.

Razorpay Pain Points
🚨

Diverted UPI wallet share

Merchants route UPI to PhonePe (0% MDR). UPI has highest NR margins for Razorpay.

📈

Revenue unpredictability

Usage-based pricing = volatile monthly revenue. No forecasting confidence.

🚫

Silent churn

Near-zero switching cost. Merchants phase out gradually — no signal until too late.

04
The Solution
Subscription Pricing Bundles:
Pay a fixed fee, get zero-fee transactions up to a limit.

Merchants choose between a free plan and paid tiers. Paid plans provide a monthly fee in exchange for zero-fee transactions up to a specific limit, and discounted MDR after exceeding it. Free credit allowance resets monthly. Annual option: pay 10 months, get 12.

Complete User Journey & Flow
The full merchant experience — when, where, and what happens at every step.
👤
Phase 1 — Signup & KYC Completion
Trigger

Merchant signs up on Razorpay, completes KYC verification. Once activated, the system checks whether this merchant falls in the 90% test group or 10% control.

⚙️ System assigns merchant to experiment variant (A/B/C/D/E) based on registered vs unregistered status
?
90% → Test Group
Shown full-screen pricing bundle landing page immediately after activation
10% → Control
No plan page shown. Standard 2% MDR applied. Proceeds directly to dashboard.
Exclusions: Cross-border, Money Saver, POS, Sub-merchant, and Partnerships merchants skip the pricing flow entirely.
📋
Phase 2 — Plan Selection Landing Page
First Touch

Full-screen pricing bundle page appears post-activation. Merchant sees Standard (Free) / Plus (₹499) / Pro (₹2,199) plans based on their assigned variant. This is the primary conversion moment.

🏁
Option A: Select Free Plan

Merchant clicks "Get Standard". Gets 5K free credits/mo, 2% or 2.5% MDR (depending on variant). Proceeds to dashboard.

⚡
Option B: Select Paid Plan (Plus/Pro)

Merchant clicks "Get Plus" or "Get Pro". Payment checkout opens — UPI Autopay, Card Auto-debit, or Mandates. On success: plan activates instantly with credits.

🚫
Option C: Payment Fails / Page Refresh

Subscription ID created but inactive. Merchant proceeds to dashboard without a plan. This is the dropoff bug — /exists API sees inactive sub, skips plan page on return.

💳 System: Free credits assigned (5K/30K/1.5L based on plan). Credits reset monthly. No rollover.
💰
Phase 3 — First 5 Transactions
Nudge Zone

Merchant starts processing payments. Free credits are consumed first (₹0 fee on first 5K/30K/1.5L GMV). After 5 transactions, contextual nudges appear for free-plan users.

🔔
Contextual Upgrade Nudge

After 5th transaction, system shows inline nudge: "You've used ₹X of your ₹5K free limit. Upgrade to Plus for 30K free credits and save on fees." Links to available-plans page.

📈
Savings Visibility

Paid plan users see cumulative savings vs standard pricing in their plan dashboard. Hidden if no credits used yet.

After 5th txn Free credits >50% used Free credits exhausted
💻
Phase 4 — Ongoing Dashboard Experience
Always On

Persistent plan visibility across the dashboard. Multiple entry points constantly remind free-plan merchants about upgrade opportunities.

🆔
Plan Badge on Logo (Always Visible)

Top nav shows "Razorpay Lite" / "Razorpay Plus" / "Razorpay Pro". Clicking takes merchant to apps/pricing-bundle/available-plan.

⭐
Golden "Get Basic" CTA

For free-plan merchants only. Golden button near profile dropdown. Redirects to apps/pricing-bundle/available-plan.

👤
Profile Dropdown

Shows currently active plan name + "Upgrade to Basic" option with benefits listed + "Manage" link to plan settings.

📊 Analytics: Track unique clicks on Logo, CTA, Dropdown → Available Plans page → Conversion to paid plan
⬆️
Phase 5 — Upgrade / Plan Change
Conversion

Merchant decides to upgrade (from entry points, nudges, or email campaigns). Payment flow initiates for the selected plan.

💳
Payment & Activation

Checkout via UPI Autopay / Card Auto-debit / Mandates. On success: new plan activates instantly. New MDR rate applied immediately.

💲
Credit Handling Rules

Free → Paid: New credits replace old (30K, not 35K). Paid → Paid: Old credits preserved + new credits added on top.

📅
Annual Option

Pay for 10 months, get 12. 200+ merchants already opted in. Email campaigns deep-link to Billing page with annual toggle pre-selected.

🛡
Phase 6 — Active Plan Lifecycle Management
Retention

Merchant manages their active plan through self-serve flows. All actions available at apps/pricing-bundle/your-plan.

📋
View Plan

Plan name, credits used/remaining, billing cycle dates, next billing amount, invoice email

💲
View Savings

Cumulative + current-cycle savings vs standard pricing. Builds value perception for renewal.

⬆️
Upgrade

Move from Plus → Pro. Instant activation. Old credits preserved + new added.

❌
Cancel

Plan stays active until cycle end. Shows exact downgrade date. Mandatory cancellation reason.

↩️
Undo Cancel

One-click undo up to 24 hours before billing cycle end. Countdown timer visible.

🔁
Auto-Renew

Credits reset monthly on billing date. Auto-debit charges next cycle. No manual action needed.

📧
Phase 7 — Re-engagement & Win-back
Growth Loop

MoEngage email campaigns trigger based on real-time events. Each cohort gets targeted messaging at the right moment.

🎯
Free → Paid Nudge (Cohort A)

Active registered merchants on free plan. Value prop: "Scale with uninterrupted service." CTA: Upgrade to Plus.

🛒
Cart Abandonment Recovery (Cohort C)

Triggered by plan_page_viewed but no subscription_success. Reassurance nudge sent within 24hrs. Deep link to plans page.

⚠️
Payment Failure Help (Cohort D)

Real-time trigger (not batch). payment_failed event → immediate email. "Help fix, don't sell." CTA: Retry Payment.

💔
Win-back (Cohort E)

subscription_downgraded event. Remind value lost + offer 2-months-free incentive. CTA: Reactivate Plan.

🚫 Immediate exclusion on subscription_success — no further campaign emails sent once merchant converts

⚠️
Edge Cases & Special Handling
Exceptions
🔧
Custom Pricing Merchants (FinOps-assigned)

System checks: Has non-default plan? → Hide all subscription UI. No pricing bundle page shown. No upgrade CTAs. No email campaigns.

🚫
Dropoff Merchants (6,996 stuck)

Inactive subscription detected. Batch migrated to free plan: Day 1 (100) → Day 2 (5,000) → Day 3 (remaining). Future: auto-assign free plan on any failure.

🔒
Subscription Check Logic

If merchant has default plan → show pricing flow. If subscription created in last 3hrs or active → skip plan page. Otherwise → assume custom, hide all UI.

💳
UPI Autopay Failure (<50% success)

Known issue. Offer Card Auto-debit and Mandates as alternatives. Payment failure triggers Cohort D email with retry options.
